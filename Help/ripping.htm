CD-Audio ripping
  
 
This window will open by pressing the button "Grab CD-Audio" in the main window of the application:


Here you can see what sound tracks are present on a disk and the information on them (time of the beginning, time of the ending, duration, the size at KB). The information is read from CD-disk inserted in specified on toolbar optical drive. You can choose other optical drive and the program automatically get the information from again chosen disk.

There is the audio a player in the bottom of the window:


With this player you directly from the program can hear the tracks chosen you and decide include them in structure of future DVD-Audio or not.

After you finally decide what tracks include in the new collection, press button "GO". The tracks chosen you will be copied on the computer. After all necessary tracks will be copied, the program will return to the main window of the application.
