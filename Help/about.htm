About Audio SixPack
  
 

It's a feeling every music lover knows: the shelves are crammed with disks but there's nothing to listen to. The songs that fit your mood at the moment — the ones that touch your soul — are scattered across a dozen different albums. And you're just too lazy to run back and forth to the player swapping disks.

Audio SixPack WILL HELP you to convert your audio CD collection into the DVD-Audio standard WITHOUT QUALITY LOSS. Using Audio SixPack you will be able to write 6 audio CDs — at the standard 44.1Khz/16bits — to 1 DVD-audio disk.

Do you really need the Audio SixPack software? Ask yourself the following questions:
  • Do you feel your CD collection takes up too much space?
  • Do you like spending time searching for the right CD?
  • Is it easy and convenient for you to repeatedly swap disks in the player?
  • Are you positive the CD now playing won't be abruptly ending?
  • Is loss of quality from the source material acceptable when you are writing your own DVDs?
  • Are you sure that the CD-Audio format is the best for storing music?
If you answered "no" to 3 or more questions, you should think about purchasing Audio SixPack.

Main features of Audio SixPack:
  • Intuitive user interface
  • Built-in CD-Audio ripping
  • Write DVD-Audio to DVD-R/W, DVD+R/W discs
  • Discs play on DVD-Audio capable players and car stereo
