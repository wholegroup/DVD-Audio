How to uninstall
  
 
Use the following steps to uninstall Audio SixPack.
  • Choose Programs -> Audio SixPack -> Uninstall from the Start menu;
  • Follow the uninstall instructions.
OR
  • Choose Settings -> Control Panel from the Start menu;
  • Open "Add/Remove" applet;
  • Select Audio SixPack item and click "Add/Remove" button;
  • Follow the uninstall instructions.
