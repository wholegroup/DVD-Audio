Main window
  
 
This window opens when you start Audio SixPack:


There are six (6) main icons in the main window of Audio SixPack:

  Icon  Description
Create the new structure of DVD-Audio (deletes all old)
Read tracks from the CD-Audio and format the next group
Remove the chosen group with all tracks in this group
Recording of generated structure DVD-Audio on DVD
Help on Audio SixPack
About Audio SixPack

Also on the main window there is the combo box in which it is possible to choose the active device (for grab or burn).


STEP ONE:

First it is necessary to generate structure of future DVD-Audio. For this purpose prepare the necessary amount of disks of CD-Audio. Choose active device for grab disks. Serially inserting disks in the optical drive and carrying out ripping the structure of your future disk will be formed. Each new CD-Audio will be seen as separate group. Format DVD-Audio determines the maximum number of groups equally 9 (NINE) and the maximum number of tracks in each group equal 99 (NINETY NINE).

STEP TWO:

After structure of your future DVD-Audio will be generated, take DVD-RW/RW on which recording will be made. Further insert it into the optical device and check up that it was active (it is chosen in the list on the toolbar). Press button "Burn DVD-Audio".
